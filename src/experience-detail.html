3D Automated Breast Ultrasound

Invenia ABUS

Low radiation Breast Cancer screening for Asian women under 40.

About 80 percent of Asian women have dense breast tissue. Both dense breast tissue and cancer appear white on a mammogram, creating a dangerous camouflage effect and a dilemma for radiologists whose goal is to find breast cancer as early as possible.

The addition of ABUS to your screening exam can provide a more comprehensive evaluation, help your physician identify any abnormalities in your tissue, and determine the next steps in your care.

Cancer can be hard to spot in Mammogram compared with ABUS.
